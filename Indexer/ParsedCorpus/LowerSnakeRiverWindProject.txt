The wind farm is being developed in phases with the first phase consisting of 149 Siemens wind turbines rated at 2.3 MW each for a maximum generating capacity of 343 MW.[1][2] The project will also create up to 150 temporary jobs and the installation will create up to 25 permanent jobs.[2][3]
